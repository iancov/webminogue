SCREENSHOT HAIKU

this is a website dedicated to a nameless art form that could be described as screenshot haiku. i will periodically and noncommittally upload interpretive haiku of the various screenshots and images found on my computer. this is intended to be a space of flexiblity and exploration into the joint medium(s) of screenshots and haiku.
